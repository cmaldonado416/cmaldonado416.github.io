My Story

I began traveling when I was very young, and not necessarily by choice. Both of my parents immigrated from the Dominican Republic to the U.S. to create a better life, but their heart was definitely back in the island. Every summer, they would gather savings to take us all back to abuelita's house.

Weaving in and out of the U.S. and growing up in Dominican culture has allowed me a unique insight into traveling. I gained a different perspective of what life is like outside of the U.S. at a very young age and as I grew older I became passionate around exploring Latin American culture. I like to explore the cultural similarities and differences, but most of all I enjoy establishing friendships and creating lasting memories.

This blog a collection of those lived experiences and learned lessons, hope you enjoy!
